Hex Realms — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 13, 2026

Hex Realms is an offline turn-based strategy game developed by NewDawn333. This policy describes how the app handles information.

Summary

We do not collect, transmit, or sell your personal data. The game runs entirely on your device. No account is required.

Data stored on your device

The app may store the following locally in your browser or app storage:

  • Game saves — in-progress matches so you can resume.
  • Custom maps — terrain you create in the map editor.
  • Settings — such as music on/off.

This data never leaves your device unless you back up your phone or browser profile through your own system tools. Uninstalling the app or clearing site data removes it.

Network access

The Android app is designed to work fully offline after install. The web version may use the network only to load the game files from GitHub Pages or your own server; gameplay itself does not require a connection.

We do not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or crash reporting in the current release.

Children

The game does not knowingly collect information from anyone. It is suitable for general audiences and contains no user-generated content shared with other players.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated page will be published at this URL with a new “Last updated” date.

Contact

Questions: open an issue at github.com/NewDawn333/hex-realms or contact the developer through your GitHub account messaging.

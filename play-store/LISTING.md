## Full description

```
Hex Realms is a turn-based strategy game on a hex grid. Expand your realm province by province, manage treasuries, raise armies, and outsmart AI opponents — all offline on your phone.

EASY TO LEARN, DEEP TO MASTER
• Tap Army, Town, or Tower, then place on highlighted hexes
• Each connected territory is a province with its own gold treasury
• Cut enemy provinces in two to cripple their economy
• Capture every rival capital to win

MAPS & MODES
• Random islands from small to gigantic (1–7 AI opponents)
• Hand-crafted scenarios: United Kingdom, California, Europe
• Built-in map editor — paint land, mountains, forests, and ferry straits
• Three difficulty levels

TACTICAL GAMEPLAY
• Four unit ranks from Militia to Champion — merge to promote
• Towns, cities, towers, and bastions with real siege rules
• Ferry straits let armies cross water without ships
• Chop forests for quick gold before they spread
• Long-press rally to move all unmoved units toward a hex

PLAY YOUR WAY
• Fully offline — no account, no ads, no data collection
• Resume saved games anytime
• Procedural music and sound (toggle in settings)
• Undo moves, replay finished matches with income charts

Perfect for quick sessions or long campaigns. Original artwork and rules — a fresh take on classic hex conquest.

Questions or feedback: github.com/NewDawn333/hex-realms
```

### Recommended screenshots to capture on your phone

After installing a release build, take portrait shots of:

1. **Main menu** — map picker visible (UK / Europe / Random)
2. **Mid-game** — map with provinces, HUD showing gold and build buttons
3. **Combat** — unit selected with move/attack highlights
4. **Map editor** — terrain tools including Strait
5. **Income chart** — tap 📊 during a game
6. **Victory or province panel** — treasury / capital detail (optional)

Save PNGs into `play-store/screenshots/` for your records. Play Console upload accepts JPEG or 24-bit PNG (no alpha), 16:9 or 9:16 aspect ratio.

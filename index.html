⬡ Hex Realms

Conquer the island, province by province.

Factions and start positions are set by the map.

How to play
  • Tap a buy button below, then tap any highlighted hex to place it (red = capture).
  • Tap a soldier, then a highlighted hex to move it. Merge soldiers to promote them.
  • Each connected blob of your color is a province with its own treasury (shown on its capital). Tap a tile to inspect one.
  • Stronger units beat weaker ones; towers and capitals protect their neighbors. Champions break any defense.
  • Soldiers cost upkeep every turn — if a province goes bankrupt, its army starves!
  • Towns and cities boost income. Chop trees for quick gold before they spread.
  • Mountains are impassable. Blue strait hexes are sea crossings — armies can ferry over them.
  • Cut an enemy province in two to cripple it. Take every capital to win.

Victory!
